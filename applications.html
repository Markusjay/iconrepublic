Applications and Projects

View all the projects being built on the ICON network.

De-Fi

Balanced

A DeFi DAO on the ICON Network that creates tokens pegged to real-world assets such as stablecoins.

OMM

A DeFi money market where anyone can lend and borrow assets, earn interest, and own a share of the protocol.

ICONFi

A crypto staking and earn service for the ICON community.

LICX

LiquidICX is a pool where people stake their ICX and receive a fully liquid LICX token as a representation of that pool.

ICX Staking Lottery

A lossless lottery on the ICON blockchain.

ICONPool

An automated liquidity provider on ICX

ICON Swap

A decentralized exchange that allows ICONists to safely trade and swap IRC2 tokens, paired with ICX.

Kotani pay

A technology stack that enables blockchain protocols to integrate seamlessly to local payment channels based primarily in Africa.

Optimus

An ICX yield optimizer and an aggregator that seeks the best interest rate between investment pools and automatically switches your fund to the more profitable one.

Utility

Zzeung

A decentralized ID app that utilizes the ICON protocol built by members of the MyID Alliance that includes industry leaders.

VisitMe

A blockchain based visitor management solution used by more than 200,000 users in Korea including major hotels, cafes and public sites.

Broof

A blockchain certificate issuance system built on the ICON protocol used by major universities and corporations such as Pohang University and Saramin.

Bridge

A payment service that lets people interact with ICON apps seamlessly using their email address and fiat currency.

ICON Vote

A voting platform that helps public representatives (P-Reps) communicate with ICX investors

Gaming & Socials

ICON Bet

A gaming DAO built on the ICON protocol. Owned and managed by the players.

Project Nebula

An NFT game of interstellar exploration. Discover and own unique planets and artifacts in an ever-expanding universe.

Stayge One

A fandom monetization platform.

FutureICX

A gamified trading dapp.

Webloc

A Tokenized Advertising Alliance Protocol.

ICON Telegram tipping bot

An ICON tipping application available on telegram.

ICON analysis & trackers

Official ICON tracker

Official icon tracker created by the ICON Foundation.

ICON vote monitor

A website for tracking delegation of P-Reps.

ICON Watch

An ICON Blockchain Explorer that allows you to explore ICON blocks, DAPPS, governance, history, and transactions.

ICON Analyst

Onchain analysis and statistics of the ICON Network.

MetrICX

A wallet that allows ICONists to track and view staking data, vote allocation, current i_Score, projected returns, and other information on mobile.

Icx stakr

ICON staking rewards calculator.

Wallets

ICONex

Official ICON wallet created by the ICON Foundation.

MyICONwallet

An ICON web wallet by the community, for the community.

Ledger

A widely used cryptocurrency wallet for assets such as ICON, Bitcoin, Ethereum and more.

Atomic Wallet

A secure cryptocurrency wallet for ICON, Bitcoin, Ethereum, and over 500 tokens.

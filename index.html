ICON

ICON is a Blockchain Protocol for Decentralized Applications and Interoperability.

Join the ICON network that includes the Seoul Government, Samsung, LINE and the world’s largest corporations.

Watch Video Learn More

Some Key ICON Usecases

Zzeung

A decentralized ID app that utilizes the ICON protocol built by members of the MyID Alliance that includes industry leaders.

VisitMe

A visitor management solution. Used by more than 200,000 users, including major hotels, cafes and public sites.

Broof

A certificate issuance app used by major universities and corporations, including Korea’s largest job marketplace, Saramin.

Balanced

A DeFi DAO on the ICON Network that creates tokens pegged to real-world assets such as stablecoins.

OMM

A DeFi money market where anyone can lend and borrow assets, earn interest, and own a share of the protocol.

ICONFi

A crypto staking and earn service for the ICON community.

Bridge

A payment service that lets people interact with ICON apps seamlessly using their email address and fiat currency.

ICON Bet

A gaming DAO built on the ICON protocol. Owned and managed by the players.

Project Nebula

An NFT game of interstellar exploration. Discover and own unique planets and artifacts in an ever-expanding universe.

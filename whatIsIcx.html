What is ICX

ICX is digital, global money.
It's the currency of ICON applications. It can be used for sending or receiving payments on the blockchain and pays for ICON transactions.

ICON Staking and Delegation

ICON currently boasts one of the highest rewards for blockchain staking.

ICX staking means locking up the ICX in your wallet for a set period of time in return for daily ICX rewards. You must delegate the staked ICX to a Public Representative (P-Rep) in order to receive these rewards.

This doesn’t mean that your ICX is sent to the P-Rep as it remains locked up in your wallet and control. The lock up period varies as network activity changes, but is currently at around 8 days.
